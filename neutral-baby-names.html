Neutral Baby Names - Project Details

Due to the high neutrality of uncommon names, this analysis focuses on names with significant representation in the population. Uncommon or fabricated names, while potentially neutral, are often less relevant to the average person.

The double bar graph reveals several key insights. Some names, like "Kris," exhibit near-perfect neutrality, while others, such as "Tommie," lean slightly toward a specific gender. However, even these skewed names remain relatively neutral, providing parents with numerous safe options for gender-neutral naming. For parents seeking a gendered name that allows for future gender identity flexibility without requiring a name change, some of these slightly skewed names could be excellent choices.

It's important to note that this graph only displays a selection of neutral names that fit within predefined tolerance levels. It does not show the full picture of how these neutral names compare to non-neutral names within the larger dataset.
